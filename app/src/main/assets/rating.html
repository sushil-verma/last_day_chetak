RATING

  • A. Truck Star Rating

    Truck Star Rating will help partner to get business enquiry on priority as compared to other less rated trucks. Below are the 5 points for each Star :-

    Registration Vehicle - Valid and update

    Insurance - Valid and Updated

    Vehicle age - less than 5 years

    GPS Device - Online tracking

    Fastag - Cashless Toll Tax
